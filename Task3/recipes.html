My Favorite Recipes

Here are some recipes that I like to cook.

Pasta with Red Sauce

A simple and delicious pasta dish with tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta in boiling water
  2. Make sauce with tomatoes and garlic
  3. Mix pasta with sauce
  4. Serve hot

Chicken with Vegetables

Healthy and tasty chicken dish with vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Broccoli
  • Carrots
  • Onion
  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Cut chicken and vegetables
  2. Fry chicken in oil
  3. Add vegetables
  4. Cook until done

Rice with Fish

A nice meal with rice and fish.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Fish
  • Lemon
  • Salt
  • Butter

Instructions

  1. Cook rice
  2. Cook fish with lemon
  3. Mix rice and fish
  4. Add butter and serve
