Pasta Bolognese

This is a traditional Italian pasta dish with meat sauce. It is very popular and tasty.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Pasta
  • Tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan
  2. Add onion and garlic
  3. Add ground beef and cook
  4. Add tomatoes and herbs
  5. Simmer for 20-30 minutes
  6. Cook pasta in boiling water
  7. Mix pasta with sauce
  8. Serve hot

Grilled Steak

A delicious grilled steak. It is a good meal for dinner. You need a good grill to cook it.

Ingredients

  • Steak (ribeye)
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Rosemary
  • Salt and pepper
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Take steak out of the fridge
  2. Dry it with paper
  3. Add salt and pepper
  4. Heat the grill
  5. Put steak on grill
  6. Cook 3-4 minutes on each side
  7. Add butter and garlic
  8. Let it rest for a few minutes

Caesar Salad

Caesar salad is a simple and fresh dish. It has lettuce, cheese and dressing. It is healthy and good.

Ingredients

  • Romaine lettuce
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Croutons
  • Garlic
  • Lemon juice
  • Mayonnaise
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Wash the lettuce
  2. Dry and cut it
  3. Make the dressing with garlic, lemon juice and mayo
  4. Add salt and pepper to the dressing
  5. Put lettuce in a bowl
  6. Pour dressing on lettuce
  7. Mix everything
  8. Add croutons and cheese on top
  9. Serve
